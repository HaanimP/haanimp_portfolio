I'm Haanim Pietersen a software developer, certified digital marketer, and co-founder of The Three Stars digital agency. My background gives me a unique edge: I understand both the creative and technical sides of growing a brand online.

I co-manage The Three Stars' Instagram presence overseeing our content direction, brand voice, and community engagement. From crafting our posting strategy to collaborating on visuals, I've been hands-on in building our agency's digital identity from the ground up.

On the project management side, I've maintained and organised complex digital projects tracking deliverables on platforms like Trello and Monday, managing documentation, keeping presentations up to date, and ensuring every team member knew what was needed and when. That same discipline and structure is what I bring to every social media account I manage.

I hold a HubSpot Inbound Marketing certification and a HubSpot Social Media Management certification internationally recognised credentials that underpin my approach to audience growth, content strategy, and measurable results.

Process

From first click to full launch

Here's exactly what working with me looks like a clear, transparent process from start to finish.

STEP 01

Explore Our Work

Start by exploring The Three Stars website to get a feel for our aesthetic, values, and the quality of work we deliver. See what we've built for other clients and picture what we could do for your brand.

Visit The Three Stars Website
5–10 minutes
STEP 02

Book a Free Consultation

Reach out via our Three Stars email to book a free 30-minute consultation. We'll chat about your brand, your goals, your audience, and what you're hoping to achieve with social media. No pressure just a conversation.

haanimatthethreestars@gmail.com
30-min call
STEP 03

Choose Your Package

After the consultation, I'll send over a personalised proposal with the package that suits your goals and budget. You review, ask questions, and once you're happy we confirm and lock in your start date.

2–3 business days
STEP 04

Brand Deep-Dive & Onboarding

I send you a detailed onboarding questionnaire covering your brand voice, visual preferences, target audience, competitors, and content do's and don'ts. This becomes the foundation of your entire strategy.

3–5 business days
STEP 05

Strategy & Content Plan Delivery

I deliver your custom content strategy complete with content pillars, posting schedule, caption tone guidelines, hashtag research, and a 30-day content calendar. You review and approve before anything goes live.

5–7 business days
STEP 06

Content Creation & Approval

I create the first batch of content graphics, captions, and scheduled posts. Everything is shared with you for review before it goes live. Revisions are included so you're always happy with what's published.

Ongoing · weekly batches
STEP 07

Go Live & Grow

Content goes live on schedule. I monitor performance, engage with your community, and track key metrics. At the end of each month, you receive a clear analytics report with insights and recommendations for the month ahead.

Ongoing · monthly reports
