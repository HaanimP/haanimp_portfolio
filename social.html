I'm Haanim Pietersen — a software developer, certified digital marketer, and co-founder of The Three Stars digital agency. My background gives me a rare edge: I understand both the creative and technical sides of growing a brand online.

I co-manage The Three Stars' Instagram presence, overseeing content direction, brand voice, and community engagement — building our agency's digital identity from the ground up, from posting strategy to visuals.

05 — Process

From first click to full launch

exactly what working with me looks like

Step 01

Explore our work & see if we're the right fit

Before reaching out, take a few minutes to browse The Three Stars website. Get a feel for our aesthetic, our values, and the quality we deliver. If it resonates, we'd love to hear from you.

Visit The Three Stars
5–10 minutes

Step 02

Book a free consultation

Reach out via our Three Stars email to book a free 30-minute consultation. We'll chat about your brand, goals, and audience — no pressure, just a conversation.

haanimatthethreestars@gmail.com
30-min call

Step 03

Choose your package

After the consultation, I'll send a personalised proposal with the package that suits your goals and budget. You review, ask questions, and once you're happy, we lock in your start date.

2–3 business days

Step 04

Brand deep-dive & onboarding

I send a detailed onboarding questionnaire covering your brand voice, visual preferences, audience, competitors, and content do's and don'ts. This becomes the foundation of your entire strategy.

3–5 business days

Step 05

Strategy & content-plan delivery

I deliver your custom strategy — content pillars, posting schedule, caption tone, hashtag research, and a 30-day content calendar. You review and approve before anything goes live.

5–7 business days

Step 06

Content creation & approval

I create the first batch of content — graphics, captions, and scheduled posts — and share everything for review before it goes live. Revisions are included so you're always happy.

Ongoing · weekly batches

Step 07

Go live & grow

Content goes live on schedule. I monitor performance, engage with your community, and track key metrics — then deliver a clear monthly report with insights and recommendations.

Ongoing · monthly reports
